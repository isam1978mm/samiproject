How the WearWell Score works

The score (0–100) is an equal blend of three sub-scores. Evidence confidence is shown separately so a clean number never hides weak data.

Comfort · physics + biologyBreathability and moisture-wicking. Ties to heat transfer (insulation traps air, lowering conduction) and thermoregulation (wicking keeps skin dry).
Skin safety · biology + chemistryDyes, finishes and fibre irritation. Starts at 100 and drops for treatments, scaled by how strong the evidence is.
Environment · climateDurability, biodegradability and microplastic shedding. Wearing items more (cost-per-wear) raises their real-world value.
